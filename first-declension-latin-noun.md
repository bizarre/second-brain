# 1st Declension Noun
* *Most* are feminine
* [[Nominative]] form usually ends in *-a*
* [[Genitive]] form usually ends in *-ae*
* Plural m/f: *-ae*
* e.g: Arena, villa, larva, nebula
* Plurals: arenas vs arenae; villas vs villae
