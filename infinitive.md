# Infinitive
* An infinitive of a [[verb]] means "to (do something)"
* e.g to think, to walk, to run, etc

## Latin
* Gives us the stem of a latin [[verb]]
* **amare**, amatus
* \+ e
* \+ prefix/suffix
* in present participle
* No change
* **ced**ere, cessus: cede
* **curr**ere, cursus: succour
