# 3rd Declension Noun
* All three genders
* Variety of endings
* Nominative form is usually from the stem
* [[Genitive]] form usually ends in *-is*
  * rex, regis
  * crux, crucis
  * miles, militis,
  * feles, felis
* Plural m/f: *-es* n: *-a*
* Plurals: matrix vs matrices; appendix vs appendices
